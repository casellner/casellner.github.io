  • Arduino IOT Simulator

    I worked with a small team in my Web Development class to build an Internet of Things simulator for a "smart chicken coop." We used Arduino controllers with humidity and temperature sensors, a motor, a motion sensor, and LEDs to get readings for the conditions of the coop and to simulate opening/closing a coop door. We utilized software taught in the class such as Node.js, HTML, Bootstrap, jQuery, and SweetAlert2 to build a RESTful API that a client could connect to and manage their SmartCoop.
